About The Tournament

This tournament was held in celebration of King Letsie III's birthday. It brought together youth from across the country in the spirit of unity, sportsmanship, and pride.

The 3x3 basketball format provided fast-paced, exciting games that showcased the incredible talent of young athletes from all regions of the country.

Beyond competition, the event served as a platform for cultural exchange and community building, reinforcing the values of teamwork and national unity.
